## Automatic Upload (Instant Mode)

### How It Works

1. Record using **Instant Mode**
2. Stop the recording
3. Video is automatically rendered and uploaded
4. Shareable link is copied to clipboard
5. Browser opens with your video (if enabled)

<Info>
  Instant Mode automatically applies optimized export settings for fast sharing.
</Info>

### Upload Progress

During upload, you'll see:

- **Rendering progress**: Frame count and percentage
- **Upload progress**: Upload percentage and speed
- **Completion**: Link copied notification

### Auto-Delete After Upload

Enable in **Settings** → **Recording** → **Delete instant recordings after upload**:

- Saves local storage space
- Keeps only cloud copy
- Local file removed after successful upload

<Warning>
  If upload fails, the local file is preserved regardless of this setting.
</Warning>

## Manual Upload from Editor

### Uploading from Studio Mode

1. Edit your recording in the desktop editor
2. Click the **Share** button in the header
3. Video is rendered with your chosen settings
4. Upload begins automatically
5. Link is copied when complete

<Steps>
  <Step title="Render">
    Video is rendered with your export settings (resolution, compression, etc.)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload">
    Rendered file is uploaded to Cap servers
  </Step>
  <Step title="Process">
    Server processes video for streaming (if needed)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Ready">
    Shareable link is generated and copied
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Upload Progress Dialog

The upload dialog shows:

```text Progress Stages
1. Preparing to render... (0%)
2. Rendering video (X/Y frames)
3. Uploading - Z%
4. Link copied to clipboard!
```

<Tip>
  Rendering progress depends on video length and export quality. Higher resolutions take longer.
</Tip>

## Reuploading Videos

Update an existing shared video:

1. Open the recording in the editor
2. Make your changes
3. Click the **reupload icon** (circular arrow)
4. Confirm reupload
5. Same shareable link is updated

<Info>
  Reuploading preserves your original share link, comments, and analytics. Viewers automatically see the new version.
</Info>

### When to Reupload

- Fix mistakes or typos in video
- Update content with new information
- Improve video quality or effects
- Add or remove sections

## Upload Requirements

### Account Requirements

<Check>Free Plan</Check>

- Upload videos up to 5 minutes
- Unlimited uploads
- Public sharing links

<Check>Cap Pro</Check>

- Upload videos of any length
- Private videos with password protection
- Custom domains
- Priority processing

### File Requirements

- **Format**: MP4 (automatically handled)
- **Codec**: H.264 video, AAC audio
- **Size**: No hard limit (limited by plan)
- **Duration**: Up to 5 minutes (Free) or unlimited (Pro)

## Upload from Dashboard

Upload videos through the web dashboard:

1. Go to [cap.so/dashboard](https://cap.so/dashboard)
2. Click **Upload** or drag and drop a video
3. Video processes automatically
4. Set title and privacy settings
5. Share your link

<Info>
  Web uploads support MP4, MOV, AVI, MKV, WEBM, WMV, M4V, and FLV formats.
</Info>

## Processing Status

After upload, videos go through processing:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Uploading">
    File is transferred to Cap servers
  </Step>
  <Step title="Processing">
    Video is optimized for streaming
  </Step>
  <Step title="Transcoding">
    Multiple quality versions are created
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete">
    Video is ready to share and watch
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Processing Time

Processing time depends on:

- Video length
- Resolution and quality
- Server load
- Your plan (Pro has priority)

<Tip>
  Most videos under 10 minutes process in 1-3 minutes.
</Tip>

## Upload Failures

### Common Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Authentication error">
    - Ensure you're signed in
    - Check that your session hasn't expired
    - Try signing out and back in
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Network timeout">
    - Check your internet connection
    - Try uploading a smaller file first
    - Disable VPN if active
    - Try again later if servers are busy
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Plan limit exceeded">
    - Video exceeds 5-minute limit on Free plan
    - Upgrade to Cap Pro for unlimited length
    - Or trim video to under 5 minutes
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="File format error">
    - Ensure video is valid MP4
    - Try exporting again from editor
    - Check that file isn't corrupted
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Retrying Uploads

If upload fails:

1. Check error message for details
2. Resolve any issues (auth, network, file)
3. Click **Share** or **Upload** again
4. Upload will resume from beginning

<Info>
  Cap does not currently support resume of partial uploads. The upload restarts if interrupted.
</Info>

## Upload from Recordings List

### Desktop App

1. Open the **Recordings** menu (video icon)
2. Find your recording
3. Click the upload icon
4. Video is rendered and uploaded

### Reupload from Recordings

For already-shared videos:

1. Find the recording in the list
2. Click the reupload icon
3. Existing share link is updated

## Upload Progress Tracking

### In Desktop App

- Progress bar shows upload percentage
- Frame rendering count (for Studio Mode)
- Estimated time remaining
- Cancellation not currently supported

### In Web Dashboard

- Upload progress indicator
- Processing status updates
- Notification when complete

## After Upload

Once upload completes:

1. **Shareable link** is copied to clipboard
2. **Browser** opens with video (if enabled in settings)
3. **Video** appears in your dashboard
4. **Notifications** sent to you (if enabled)

### Accessing Your Video

Find uploaded videos:

- **Desktop**: Recordings list shows upload status
- **Web**: Dashboard at [cap.so/dashboard](https://cap.so/dashboard)
- **Direct link**: Use copied shareable link

## Network Considerations

### Upload Speed

Upload time depends on:

- Your internet upload speed
- Video file size
- Server location
- Network congestion

<Tip>
  For faster uploads, connect via ethernet instead of Wi-Fi when possible.
</Tip>

### Bandwidth Usage

Typical upload sizes:

- **1080p, 5 minutes**: ~200-500 MB
- **1440p, 5 minutes**: ~400-800 MB  
- **4K, 5 minutes**: ~800-1500 MB

Lower compression quality increases file size.

## Privacy During Upload

- Videos are uploaded over **HTTPS** (encrypted)
- Only you can access until you share the link
- Default videos are **unlisted** (link required to view)
- Set videos to **private** with password (Pro)

<Info>
  Your videos are private by default. They're only accessible to those with the share link.
</Info>
