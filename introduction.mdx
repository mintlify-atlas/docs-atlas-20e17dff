# Welcome to Cap

Cap is the open source alternative to Loom. It's a video messaging tool that allows you to record, edit and share videos in seconds.

<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/CapSoftware/Cap/refs/heads/main/apps/web/public/landing-cover.png" alt="Cap Screenshot" />

## Why Cap?

Cap combines the power of professional screen recording with the simplicity of modern design. Built with privacy in mind, Cap gives you full control over your recordings while providing features that rival commercial alternatives.

## Key Features

### Recording

- **Screen & Window Capture**: Record your entire screen or specific application windows
- **System Audio**: Capture system audio alongside your recordings
- **Camera Support**: Built-in webcam recording with picture-in-picture layout
- **High Performance**: Built with Rust and modern web technologies for smooth recording

### Editing

- **Video Trimming**: Cut and trim your recordings with precision
- **Built-in Editor**: Edit videos without leaving the app
- **Quick Processing**: Fast video processing powered by FFmpeg

### Sharing

- **Shareable Links**: Generate instant links to your recordings
- **Custom Branding**: Personalize your video pages with custom branding
- **Analytics Dashboard**: Track views, engagement, and viewer insights powered by Tinybird
- **Access Control**: Manage who can view your recordings

### AI Features

- **Auto Transcription**: Automatic speech-to-text transcription powered by Deepgram
- **AI Summaries**: Generate concise summaries of your video content
- **Searchable Content**: Make your recordings searchable through transcripts

## Technology Stack

Cap is built with modern, performant technologies:

- **Desktop App**: Tauri (Rust) with SolidStart frontend
- **Web Platform**: Next.js 15 with React 19
- **Backend**: Effect (functional TypeScript), Drizzle ORM
- **Database**: MySQL 8.0
- **Storage**: S3-compatible (AWS S3, MinIO)
- **Infrastructure**: Docker, Docker Compose
