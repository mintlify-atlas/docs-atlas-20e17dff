## AI Summaries Overview

<Check>Cap Pro Required</Check>

Cap Pro includes AI-powered video analysis that automatically generates:

- **Video summaries**: Concise overview of your video content
- **Chapter markers**: Intelligent segmentation with titles
- **Key points**: Bullet-point highlights
- **Auto titles**: Suggested video titles based on content

<Info>
  AI summaries use advanced language models to understand and summarize your video based on the transcript.
</Info>

## How AI Summaries Work

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upload Video">
    Upload a video with audio to Cap
  </Step>
  <Step title="Transcription">
    Cap generates an accurate transcript of the audio
  </Step>
  <Step title="AI Analysis">
    AI analyzes the transcript to understand content and context
  </Step>
  <Step title="Summary Generation">
    AI creates a summary, chapters, and key points
  </Step>
  <Step title="Ready">
    Summary appears in the Summary tab (usually 1-3 minutes)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Viewing AI Summaries

### Accessing the Summary

1. Open your video share page
2. Click the **Summary** tab in the sidebar
3. View AI-generated content:
   - Text summary
   - Chapter list with timestamps

### Summary Components

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Summary">
    A concise paragraph (2-4 sentences) summarizing the main points of your video.
    
    **Example:**
    > This tutorial demonstrates how to use Cap Desktop for screen recording. It covers setup, recording modes, and basic editing features. Perfect for beginners getting started with Cap.
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Chapters">
    Time-stamped sections with descriptive titles.
    
    **Example:**
    - 00:00 - Introduction to Cap
    - 01:23 - Setting Up Your Recording
    - 03:45 - Recording Your Screen
    - 06:12 - Basic Editing
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Video Summaries

### What's Included

AI summaries provide:

- **Main topic**: What the video is about
- **Key points**: Most important information
- **Context**: Background or setup information
- **Conclusion**: Main takeaway or call-to-action

### Summary Format

Summaries use Markdown formatting:

- **Bold** for emphasis
- Bullet points for lists
- Paragraphs for readability

### Summary Quality

**Best results with:**

- Clear, well-structured content
- Distinct topics or sections
- 3+ minutes of speech
- Single speaker or organized dialogue

**May be skipped if:**

- Video is too short (< 30 seconds of speech)
- No clear speech detected
- Extremely fragmented content

## AI Chapters

### Auto-Generated Chapters

Chapters segment your video into logical sections:

- **Timestamps**: Exact start time for each chapter
- **Titles**: Descriptive chapter names
- **Clickable**: Jump directly to any chapter

### Chapter Navigation

Chapters appear in:

1. **Summary Tab**: Full chapter list
2. **Video Player**: Chapter markers in progress bar
3. **Embed Players**: Available in embedded videos

<Tip>
  Viewers can click chapter titles to jump directly to that section, making long videos easier to navigate.
</Tip>

## Generation Status

AI summaries have several states:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Queued">
    Waiting to be processed. Usually brief (less than 1 minute).
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Processing">
    AI is analyzing your video. Typically 1-3 minutes.
    
    **What you see:** Loading skeleton with animated placeholder
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Complete">
    Summary is ready to view.
    
    **What you see:** Full summary and chapters
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Skipped">
    Video was too short or had insufficient speech.
    
    **What you see:** "The video was too short or had no speech detected"
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Error">
    Generation failed. Retry button available.
    
    **What you see:** Error message with retry option
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Retry AI Generation

If generation fails or was skipped:

1. Open the Summary tab
2. Click **Retry AI Generation** button
3. Wait for processing (1-3 minutes)
4. Summary appears when complete

<Warning>
  Retrying replaces any existing AI-generated content. Manual edits may be lost.
</Warning>

## Privacy and Data

### What AI Processes

- Video transcript (text only)
- Timestamp information
- No video or audio files are analyzed directly

### Data Storage

- AI summaries are stored with your video
- Can be regenerated or deleted anytime
- Not used for AI model training
- Respects video privacy settings

<Info>
  AI analysis happens on Cap's servers. Your transcript data is processed securely and is not shared with third parties.
</Info>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="'No summary available' message">
    - Video may be too short (< 30 sec of speech)
    - Transcription may have failed
    - Check that Cap Pro is active
    - Try clicking Retry AI Generation
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Summary stuck at 'Processing'">
    - Wait 3-5 minutes (processing time varies)
    - Refresh the page
    - If stuck after 5 minutes, retry generation
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Summary doesn't match video content">
    - Check transcript accuracy first
    - Edit transcript and regenerate summary
    - Manually edit summary in video settings
    - Contact support if consistently inaccurate
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Chapters are incorrect">
    - Regenerate with edited transcript
    - Manual chapter editing coming soon
    - Provide feedback to improve AI
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="'Skipped' status">
    - Video is too short for meaningful summary
    - Insufficient speech detected in video
    - Try longer videos with clear narration
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
